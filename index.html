About me

I am a native of Suqian, Jiangsu Province. I studied in Huaiyin Middle School of Jiangsu Province from 2016 to 2019 (I entered the Jiangsu Provincial Physics Olympiad team in 2018). I have been studying in Zhiyuan College of Shanghai Jiao Tong University for an honorary degree in physics from 2019 to now, and served in the army from 2020 to 2022. In the future, I will study for a doctorate degree in computer science in the School of Artificial Intelligence of Shanghai Jiao Tong University under the supervision of Junchi Yan.

Recent News

[25/11/24] The 19th "Challenge Cup" National College Students' Extra-Curricular Academic Science and Technology Works Competition 2024 final final of the special competition has recently ended. In the topic of "The Fusion of Quantum and Supercomputer Creating the Future - Application Exploration of Quantum Classical Hybrid Algorithm", the project "Constrained Variational Quantum Algorithm with application in quantum chemistry" by Wang Ruocheng from Zhiyuan College, Xia Zhuo from the Institute of Electricity and Wang Yuhan from Zhiyuan College won the first prize.
